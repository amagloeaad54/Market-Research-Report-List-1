The single-blade system features one blade and a dedicated chamber for ink, enabling precise control but limited flexibility. In contrast, the dual-blade system incorporates two blades, allowing for better adjustment of ink thickness and distribution while improving overall print quality. Both systems enhance efficiency, reduce waste, and provide consistent results, catering to different printing applications and production needs.</p></p>
