<p><p>The varnish remover market is categorized into three main types: Acid Varnish Remover, Alkaline Varnish Remover, and Neutral Varnish Remover. Alkaline varnish removers use alkaline substances to dissolve varnish, making them suitable for various surfaces. Neutral varnish removers offer a balanced pH, providing a safer option for delicate applications without aggressive chemical reactions, catering to a wider range of users.</p></p>
