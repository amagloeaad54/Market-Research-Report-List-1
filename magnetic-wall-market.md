<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnetic Wall</li><li>Electromagnetic Wall</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnetic Wall market is divided into two primary types: Permanent Magnetic Walls and Electromagnetic Walls. Permanent Magnetic Walls utilize fixed magnets to create a stable magnetic field, suitable for applications that require constant magnetism without power consumption. In contrast, Electromagnetic Walls rely on electrical currents to generate a magnetic field, allowing for adjustable strength and applications that may need variable functionality. Both types serve distinct purposes in industries like manufacturing, construction, and material handling, enhancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
