<p><p>FRP (Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic) pipe jacking is a trenchless installation technique used for the laying of pipelines where traditional excavation methods are not feasible or are too disruptive. This process involves the use of a hydraulic jack to push prefabricated FRP pipes through the ground, minimizing surface disruption and environmental impact. FRP pipes are known for their corrosion resistance, lightweight properties, and durability, making them suitable for various applications, including sewage, drainage, and industrial pipelines.</p><p>The FRP Pipe Jacking Market is poised for robust growth, driven by the increasing demand for sustainable construction methods and the need for infrastructure upgrades in urban areas.
