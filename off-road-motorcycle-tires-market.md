<p><ul><li>Hard Terrain Tires</li><li>Intermediate Terrain Tires</li><li>Soft Terrain Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Off-road motorcycle tires are categorized into three primary types based on the terrain they are designed for. Hard terrain tires feature a durable rubber compound and a tightly spaced tread pattern, ideal for rocky and hard-packed surfaces, providing optimal traction and stability. Intermediate terrain tires offer versatility with a balanced tread design suitable for a mix of hard and soft surfaces. Soft terrain tires have deeper, wider treads tailored for loose and muddy conditions, enhancing grip and maneuverability in challenging environments.</p></p>
