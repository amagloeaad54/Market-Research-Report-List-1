<p><p>The air quality sensor market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on signal-gas monitoring sensors and multi-gas monitoring sensors. In contrast, multi-gas monitoring sensors can simultaneously measure multiple air pollutants, offering comprehensive data for real-time air quality assessment. These sensors are crucial for environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and public health initiatives, driving advancements in smart technology and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
