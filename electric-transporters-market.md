<p><p>The electric transporters market features various battery types, primarily Sealed Lead Acid (SLA), Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH), and Lithium-Ion (Li-Ion). SLA batteries are cost-effective and widely used in low-power applications but have limited lifespan and energy density. NiMH batteries offer higher capacity and better performance than SLA, making them suitable for hybrid vehicles. Li-Ion batteries dominate the market due to their lightweight, high energy density, and longer cycle life, driving advancements in electric transport technologies.</p></p>
