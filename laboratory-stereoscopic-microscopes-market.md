<p><p>Laboratory stereoscopic microscopes are essential tools for 3D visualization of specimens. The monocular stereoscopic microscope features a single eyepiece, making it compact and user-friendly for quick inspections. The binocular variant offers two eyepieces, enhancing depth perception and comfort during prolonged use. The trinocular stereoscopic microscope includes an additional optical path, allowing for the attachment of a camera or imaging equipment, facilitating documentation and analysis alongside direct observation. Each type serves specialized needs in various laboratory applications.</p></p>
