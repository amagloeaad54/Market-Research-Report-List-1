<p><p>The teeth whitening light market consists of three main types: LED, UV, and infrared lights. LED lights are popular for their efficiency and safety, accelerating the whitening process without damaging the tooth enamel. UV lights, though effective, raise concerns about potential harm to gums and overall health due to UV exposure. Infrared lights offer a gentler approach, working by penetrating deeper into the teeth to enhance whitening effects while minimizing heat and sensitivity. Each type provides unique benefits for consumers.</p></p>
