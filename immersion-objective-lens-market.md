<p><p>The immersion objective lens market comprises various types, primarily categorized into oil immersion and water immersion lenses, along with other specialized lenses. Oil immersion objective lenses utilize immersion oil to enhance resolution and light transmission, making them ideal for studying fine details in samples at higher magnifications. Water immersion objective lenses, on the other hand, use water as an immersion medium, suitable for live cell imaging and biological applications due to their refractive index compatibility. Other types may include specialized lenses for specific applications.</p></p>
