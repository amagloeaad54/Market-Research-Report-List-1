<p><strong>The Protein Production Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prokaryotic Expression Systems</li><li>Mammalian Cell Expression Systems</li><li>Yeast Cell Expression Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protein production markets are categorized based on expression systems used to produce proteins. Prokaryotic expression systems, such as bacteria, are cost-effective and fast but may lack post-translational modifications. Mammalian cell expression systems offer complex protein structures, suited for therapeutic proteins, but are more costly and slower. Yeast cell expression systems strike a balance, providing moderate cost and post-translational modifications. Additionally, "Others" encompass alternative systems like insect cells or cell-free systems, catering to specific production needs and variabilities.</p></p>
