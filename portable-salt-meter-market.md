<p><strong>The Portable Salt Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Type</li><li>Conductivity Type</li><li>Hydrometer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable salt meter market is categorized into three main types: Optical Type, Conductivity Type, and Hydrometer Type. Optical type meters measure salt concentration by analyzing light absorption or refraction in a solution. Conductivity meters assess the ability of a solution to conduct electrical current, which correlates with salt levels. Hydrometers gauge specific gravity to infer salt concentration based on buoyancy principles. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries, such as food, agriculture, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
