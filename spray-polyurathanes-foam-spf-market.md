<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) is a versatile insulation material available in three main types: Open-Cell (low density), Closed-Cell (medium density), and Closed-Cell (high density). Open-Cell foam is lightweight, more flexible, and provides excellent sound insulation, making it ideal for interior applications. Closed-Cell foam, available in medium and high density, offers superior structural strength, moisture resistance, and thermal insulation, making it suitable for exterior applications and demanding environments. Each type serves specific insulation needs across various industries.</p></p>
