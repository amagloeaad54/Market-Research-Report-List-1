<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wired Control Vehicle Barrier Gates are categorized into two main types: Straight and Crank. Straight barrier gates consist of a horizontal arm that pivots from one end, providing a straightforward and efficient entry control solution. In contrast, Crank barrier gates use a mechanical crank mechanism to raise and lower the barrier arm, offering enhanced flexibility in positioning and operation. Both types serve to regulate vehicle access in various settings, including commercial lots and secured facilities, improving security and traffic flow.</p></p>
