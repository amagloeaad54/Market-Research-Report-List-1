<p><p>Rapid prototyping in the aerospace and defense market utilizes various advanced manufacturing techniques to create functional models quickly and efficiently. Stereolithography (SLA) uses ultraviolet light to cure liquid resin, allowing for high precision. Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM) layers adhesive-coated material to form parts. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) fuses powdered materials with a laser for durability. Three-Dimensional Printing (3DP) applies powdered materials layer by layer. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) extrudes thermoplastic filaments, enabling cost-effective production of prototypes and parts.</p></p>
