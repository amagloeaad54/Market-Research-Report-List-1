<p><ul><li>RAW Editing Software</li><li>Non-RAW Editing Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photo editor market encompasses two primary types: RAW editing software and non-RAW editing software. RAW editing software caters to professional photographers, allowing them to manipulate uncompressed image files for greater control over exposure, color balance, and details. This type is essential for high-quality prints and extensive post-processing. In contrast, non-RAW editing software is user-friendly and targets casual users, offering basic editing features like cropping, filters, and adjustments for edited JPEGs or PNGs, making it accessible for everyday tasks.</p></p>
