<p><p>The ArF scanner market is primarily categorized into two types: ArF immersion scanners and dry ArF scanners. ArF immersion scanners utilize a liquid medium to enhance resolution by reducing the wavelength of light used in lithography, allowing for finer patterning on semiconductor wafers. In contrast, dry ArF scanners operate in air without immersion fluids, typically used for larger feature sizes or less critical applications. Both types play crucial roles in advanced semiconductor manufacturing, each suited for specific production needs and technologies.</p></p>
