<p><ul><li>Geodetic Measuring Devices</li><li>Geotechnical Measuring Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geotechnical deformation monitoring devices market encompasses two main types: geodetic measuring devices and geotechnical measuring devices. Geodetic measuring devices, such as GPS and total stations, focus on large-scale land deformation monitoring, offering precise spatial measurements. In contrast, geotechnical measuring devices, like inclinometers and piezometers, are specialized for monitoring subsurface conditions and structural integrity, assessing factors like soil movement and groundwater levels. Together, these devices provide essential data for infrastructure safety and environmental management.</p></p>
