<p><p>The reinforced surgical gown market comprises three main types: SMS surgical gowns, spunlace surgical gowns, and others. SMS surgical gowns are made from a combination of spunbond and meltblown materials, offering breathability and liquid resistance. Spunlace surgical gowns utilize a non-woven fabric created through hydroentanglement, providing softness and comfort while maintaining barrier protection. Other gowns may include various materials and designs that meet specific clinical needs. These options enhance safety and comfort for healthcare professionals during surgical procedures.</p></p>
