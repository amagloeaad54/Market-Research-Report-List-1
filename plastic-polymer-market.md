<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Polymers</li><li>Thermosetting Polymers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic polymer market is broadly categorized into thermoplastic and thermosetting polymers. Thermoplastic polymers are materials that can be repeatedly melted and molded when heated, making them versatile for various applications such as packaging, automotive, and consumer goods. In contrast, thermosetting polymers undergo a chemical change when heated, resulting in a rigid structure that cannot be remolded. These are commonly used in applications requiring high durability and heat resistance, such as electronics, adhesives, and coatings.</p></p>
