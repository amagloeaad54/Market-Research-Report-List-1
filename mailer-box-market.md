<p><ul><li>Single-Walled</li><li>Double Walled</li><li>Triple Walled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mailer boxes are categorized into three types based on their wall construction: single-walled, double-walled, and triple-walled. Single-walled mailer boxes are lightweight and suitable for lightweight products, offering basic protection. Double-walled mailer boxes provide added strength, making them ideal for heavier items or those requiring extra security during transit. Triple-walled mailer boxes offer maximum durability and protection for fragile, heavy, or high-value products, ensuring they withstand rough handling and potential damage during shipping. Each type caters to specific shipping and packaging needs.</p></p>
