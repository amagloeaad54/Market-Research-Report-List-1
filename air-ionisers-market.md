<p><p>The air ionisers market primarily comprises two types: those that generate negative ions and balanced ion generators. Negative ion generators release negatively charged ions into the air, which attach to pollutants and allergens, effectively neutralizing them and improving air quality. Balanced ion generators, on the other hand, produce both positive and negative ions to create a balanced ionic atmosphere, which can enhance air purification while promoting a more comfortable indoor environment. Both types aim to improve air quality and overall well-being.</p></p>
