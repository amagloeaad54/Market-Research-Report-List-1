<p><strong>The Back Grinding Tapes (BGT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Type</li><li>Non-UV Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Back Grinding Tapes (BGT) are essential in the semiconductor manufacturing process, used to protect wafers during the back grinding process. The market is divided into two main types: UV Type and Non-UV Type. UV Type tapes utilize ultraviolet light to enable easy removal after grinding, ensuring minimal residue and damage to the wafer. In contrast, Non-UV Type tapes do not rely on UV light for removal and are often used in applications where higher durability and heat resistance are required.</p></p>
