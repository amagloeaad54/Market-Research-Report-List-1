<p><strong>The Baggage Conveyors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt</li><li>Roller</li><li>Destination-coded Vehicle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baggage conveyors market comprises three primary types: belt conveyors, roller conveyors, and destination-coded vehicles. Belt conveyors are designed for smooth, continuous transport of luggage and are commonly used in airports. Roller conveyors utilize a series of rollers to facilitate movement, providing flexibility and efficiency for varying baggage sizes. Destination-coded vehicles, equipped with automated systems, transport bags to specific locations based on coded tags, enhancing sorting and speeding up the baggage handling process. Each type plays a crucial role in streamlining airport operations.</p></p>
