<p><strong>The Non-embedded Pico Projector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS)</li><li>Laser Beam Steering (LBS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-embedded pico projector market consists of three main types: Digital Light Processing (DLP), Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS), and Laser Beam Steering (LBS). DLP technology uses micro-mirrors to produce bright images, favored for its efficiency and color accuracy. LCoS employs liquid crystals for high-resolution displays, ideal for detailed visuals. LBS utilizes laser technology for precise image projection, offering compact designs and rapid image shifting. Each type caters to specific needs in portability and image quality.</p></p>
