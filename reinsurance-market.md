<p><p>The reinsurance market is primarily divided into two segments: Property and Casualty (P&C) reinsurance and Life reinsurance. P&C reinsurance covers risks related to property damage and liability, helping insurers manage exposure from events like natural disasters or accidents. In contrast, Life reinsurance deals with life insurance policies, addressing risks associated with mortality and longevity. Both markets enable primary insurers to stabilize their finances, enhance capacity to underwrite risks, and maintain solvency amid unforeseen claims.</p></p>
