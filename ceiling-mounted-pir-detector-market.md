<p><p>The ceiling-mounted PIR detector market encompasses three primary types: occupancy detectors, presence detectors, and motion detectors. Occupancy detectors activate lighting or systems based on the presence of individuals in a designated area, ideal for energy efficiency. Presence detectors go further by detecting subtle movements, ensuring spaces are illuminated even when people are still. Motion detectors primarily sense larger movements, triggering alerts or responses for security purposes. Together, these devices enhance safety, energy efficiency, and convenience in various environments.</p></p>
