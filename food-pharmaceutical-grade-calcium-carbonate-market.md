<p><p>The Food and Pharmaceutical Grade Calcium Carbonate market includes two main types: Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC) and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC). GCC is produced through mechanical means, resulting in a coarser texture suitable for applications like dietary supplements and food additives. In contrast, PCC is chemically synthesized, offering a finer purity and tailored properties, making it ideal for pharmaceuticals and high-end food products. Both types cater to stringent regulatory standards, ensuring safety and efficacy in various applications.</p></p>
