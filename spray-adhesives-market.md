<p><strong>The Spray Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li><li>Hot Melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray adhesives market encompasses three primary types: solvent-based, water-based, and hot melt adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, offering strong bonding and versatility but may pose health and environmental risks. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, providing a safer option with reduced emissions and easy cleanup, making them popular in eco-conscious applications. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and melt when heated, offering rapid bonding and strong adhesion for various materials, ideal for industrial and craft uses.</p></p>
