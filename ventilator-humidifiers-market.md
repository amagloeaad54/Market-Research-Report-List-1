<p><ul><li>Bubble Humidifiers</li><li>Electronic Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ventilator humidifiers market features two primary types: bubble humidifiers and electronic humidifiers. Bubble humidifiers work by forcing air through a water reservoir, creating bubbles that increase humidity before the air reaches the patient. In contrast, electronic humidifiers utilize advanced technology to control temperature and humidity levels with precision, providing optimal moisture without causing condensation. Both types are essential for respiratory care, enhancing patient comfort and reducing airway irritation during mechanical ventilation.</p></p>
